Home Cleaning
$14.50 – 15.50 Per Hour – Part –time and Casual

Monday – Friday/Weekends and Statutory Holidays
Responsibilities
  • Dusting, Mopping, Vacuuming
  • Cleaning of the kitchen, bathroom, and other living areas
  • Making beds and doing laundry
  • Light meals preparation and grocery shopping
  • Monitoring and documenting home environment for any hazards
  • Performing other duties as required
Qualifications
  • A minimum of 1 year working experience in providing cleaning services
  • Experience working with a vulnerable population (Seniors and Adults with complex medical conditions
  • Physical ability to perform light cleaning (e.g. washing floors, dusting, laundry) and knowledge of basic health and safety standards.
  • First Aid/CPR is required
  • Knowledge of routine precautions required for cleanliness and disinfecting and for the control of the spread of communicable diseases
    • Ability to lift up to 50 pounds
  • Immunization up-to-date
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills
  • Effective problem-solver
  • Completion of a current police check and a vulnerable sector check
  • Computer literate: email and internet
  • Second language an asset
  • Access to vehicle an asset

Only candidates selected for an interview will be contacted. Please note that in accordance with the Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act (AODA), Reliable Support Services will provide accommodation in an accessible way for applicants upon request.
