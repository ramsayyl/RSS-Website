Homemaking

Our Personal Support Workers provide assistance with personal care in the clients private homes, retirement homes, hospitals or long-term care homes. We assist with activities of daily living (ADL) such as: bathing, personal hygiene, bed mobility, transferring, walking, dressing, eating and toileting, etc. We also assist with instrumental activities of daily living (IADL), such as: meal preparation and grocery shopping.

Home Cleaning

We provide cleaning to clients which includes, laundry and light housekeeping (dusting, mopping, vacuuming and making the bed).
