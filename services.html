Personal Support Services

Our Personal Support Workers provide assistance with personal care in the clients private homes, retirement homes, hospitals or long-term care homes. We assist with activities of daily living (ADL).

Home Cleaning

We provide cleaning to clients which includes, laundry and light housekeeping (dusting, mopping, vacuuming and making the bed).

Service Restrictions

Personal Support Workers are not permitted to...

  • Perform any acts that are restricted under the Regulated Health Care Professions Act
  • Perform activities that are not considered routine act of daily living
  • Complete any task that requires the use of a step ladder or ladder
  • Shave client using a razor blade or cut hair
  • Provide foot-cutting toenails or fingernails
  • Change wound dressings
  • Provide pet care
  • Provide assistance with transfers for non weight bearing individuals
  • Provide and/or charge for private services outside of the client’s service(s) with the agency

Home Cleaners are not permitted to...

  • Scrub, strip or wax floors
  • Move heavy furniture
  • Complete any task that requires the use of a step ladder
  • Complete any tasks that required them to be on hands and knees
  • Polish silver
  • Steam clean carpets
  • Remove or clean heavy drapery or blinds
  • Wash ceilings, walls or windows
  • Assist with personal care
